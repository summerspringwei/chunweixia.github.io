I'm excited to accept the invitation to serve as a **Reviewer** for the **AI4Sciences Track** at **KDD 2026**.

## Conference Details

- **Conference**: KDD 2026 (ACM SIGKDD Conference on Knowledge Discovery and Data Mining)
- **Track**: AI for Sciences (First Edition)
- **Date**: August 9-13, 2026
- **Location**: Jeju, Korea

## Track Overview

The **AI for Sciences Track** is a newly introduced track highlighting the increasingly important roles of AI and data-driven methods for interdisciplinary research and scientific discoveries. This inaugural track represents KDD's commitment to fostering AI applications beyond traditional data science and business domains.

## Submission Information

- **Submission Cycle**: Second Cycle (February 2026)
- **Submissions to AI4Sciences Track**: Only accepted in the Second Cycle
- **Note**: Papers from other tracks in the Second Cycle are not eligible for the AI4Sciences Track, but papers rejected from the First Cycle can be resubmitted if they are highly related to AI for Sciences.

This is an exciting opportunity to contribute to this emerging field and help advance the intersection of AI and scientific discovery!
