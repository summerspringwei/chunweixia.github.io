I'm honored to be invited to serve as a **Program Committee Member** for the **Systems and Infrastructure for Web, Mobile, and Web of Things** track at **The Web Conference 2026**.

## Conference Details

- **Conference**: The ACM Web Conference 2026 (WWW 2026)
- **Date**: April 13-17, 2026
- **Location**: Dubai, United Arab Emirates
- **Track**: Systems and Infrastructure for Web, Mobile, and Web of Things
- **Track Chairs**: Petteri Nurmi and Zheng Wang

The Web Conference is the premier annual forum for advances in research, development, standards, and applications shaping the future of the Web. I'm excited to contribute to this prestigious conference and help advance research in web systems and infrastructure!
