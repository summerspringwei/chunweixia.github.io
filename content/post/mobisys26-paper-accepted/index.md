## About the Work

This paper addresses an important challenge in deploying large language models on mobile and edge devices with limited memory. We propose KVSwap, a novel approach for disk-aware KV cache offloading that enables efficient long-context inference on resource-constrained devices.

MobiSys is a premier venue for innovative research on mobile computing, applications, and services. We're excited to present this work and contribute to advancing on-device AI capabilities!
